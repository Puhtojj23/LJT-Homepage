## Abstract

We introduce C-Eval, a comprehensive multi-level, multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models. C-Eval covers a wide range of subjects and difficulty levels, providing a robust benchmark for evaluating Chinese language capabilities in large models.
